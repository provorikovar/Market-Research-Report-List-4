<p><p>Laser theodolite market includes two main types: transit theodolites and non-transit theodolites. Transit theodolites are more traditional and commonly used for surveying and construction projects. They have a telescope that can rotate horizontally and vertically, allowing for accurate angle measurements. Non-transit theodolites, on the other hand, are more advanced and often incorporate laser technology for precise measurements. They are commonly used in engineering and research applications where accuracy is crucial. Both types play important roles in various industries that require precise angle and distance measurements.</p></p>
