<p><strong>The Automotive Oil Pressure Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sliding-Stem Globe Type</li><li>V-Notch Ball Type</li><li>Butterfly Type</li><li>Angle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive oil pressure control valves come in four main types: Sliding-Stem Globe Type, V-Notch Ball Type, Butterfly Type, and Angle Type. Sliding-Stem Globe Type valves regulate oil flow using a linear motion stem. V-Notch Ball Type valves have a ball with a V-shaped notch that controls oil flow. Butterfly Type valves use a rotating disc to regulate flow, while Angle Type valves have an angled body to redirect oil flow. Each type of valve is designed to fulfill specific requirements in automotive oil pressure control systems.</p></p>
