<p><p>Specialty synthetic graphite market types include isotropic graphite, extruded graphite, molded graphite, and others. Isotropic graphite is a high-density, fine-grain graphite material with consistent properties in all directions. Extruded graphite is formed by pressing and extruding graphite powder into the desired shape. Molded graphite is manufactured by compressing graphite powder in a mold. Other types of specialty synthetic graphite may include expanded graphite or flexible graphite, which are used in various industrial applications due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
