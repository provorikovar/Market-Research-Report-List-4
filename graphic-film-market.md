<p><p>The graphic film market can be broadly classified into different types based on the materials used. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) film is known for its durability and flexibility, making it a popular choice for signage and labeling. Polypropylene film offers excellent clarity and printability, making it ideal for packaging and labeling applications. Polyethylene film is commonly used for temporary signage and outdoor advertising due to its weather resistance. Other markets in the graphic film industry include materials such as polyester film, polycarbonate film, and acrylic film, each offering unique properties and applications.</p></p>
