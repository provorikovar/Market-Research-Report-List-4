Web-based CTMS solutions are accessed through a web browser and require internet connectivity for use. Cloud-based CTMS solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On-Premise CTMS solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing complete control over data security and customization. Each type of market solution caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
