<p><p>Radial piston pumps are commonly classified into two main types: single-stage pump and multi-stage pump. Single-stage pumps have one set of pistons that operate within a single chamber, providing a single level of pressure. On the other hand, multi-stage pumps have multiple sets of pistons that work in separate chambers, enabling them to provide higher pressure levels. These different types cater to varying applications and industries, offering a wide range of choices for customers based on their specific needs.</p></p>
