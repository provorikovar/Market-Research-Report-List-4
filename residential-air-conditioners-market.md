<p><ul><li>Compressor</li><li>Condenser</li><li>Metering Device</li><li>Cooling Coil</li><li>Air Handling Unit</li><li>Duct System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential air conditioners consist of various components such as the compressor, condenser, metering device, cooling coil, air handling unit, and duct system. The compressor is responsible for pressurizing the refrigerant, while the condenser releases heat. The metering device regulates the flow of refrigerant, and the cooling coil absorbs heat from the air. The air handling unit distributes cooled air, and the duct system helps to circulate the air throughout the home. Each component plays a crucial role in cooling and maintaining a comfortable indoor environment.</p></p>
