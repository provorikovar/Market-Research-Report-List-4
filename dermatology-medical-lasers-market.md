<p><strong>The Dermatology Medical Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surgical Lasers</li><li>Vascular Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical lasers are used in dermatology for procedures such as skin resurfacing, tattoo removal, and hair removal. These lasers are designed to cut, coagulate, or vaporize tissue with high precision and minimal damage to surrounding skin. Vascular lasers, on the other hand, target blood vessels and are commonly used to treat conditions like spider veins, port wine stains, and rosacea. These lasers work by selectively heating and destroying blood vessels while leaving surrounding skin unharmed.</p></p>
