<p><strong>The Laboratory Spray Drying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Spray Dryer</li><li>Stream Spray Dryer</li><li>Centrifuge Spray Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory spray drying machines are used for drying liquid materials into powders. There are three types of spray dryers commonly used in the market. Pressure spray dryers use high pressure nozzles to atomize the liquid, which is then dried quickly. Steam spray dryers use hot steam to dry the liquid material. Centrifuge spray dryers use a spinning chamber to separate the liquid from the material, resulting in quick drying. Each type of spray dryer has its own unique advantages and applications in laboratory settings.</p></p>
