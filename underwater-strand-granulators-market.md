<p><p>Underwater strand granulators are available in two main types - manual and automatic. Manual granulators require operators to manually feed the plastic strands into the machine and control the cutting process. On the other hand, automatic granulators are equipped with sensors and automated systems that feed the strands into the machine and control the cutting process without the need for manual intervention. Both types of granulators cater to different market needs and preferences, offering options for varying levels of automation and control.</p></p>
