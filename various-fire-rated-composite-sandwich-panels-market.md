<p><p>Fire rated composite sandwich panels are classified based on their fire resistance capabilities. A2 fire rated panels are the most fire-resistant, followed by B, C, D, E, and F rated panels in descending order. These classifications indicate the time the panels can withstand fire before combustion occurs. A2 rated panels offer the highest level of protection, making them suitable for high-risk environments. Conversely, F rated panels have the lowest fire resistance and are best suited for low-risk applications.</p></p>
