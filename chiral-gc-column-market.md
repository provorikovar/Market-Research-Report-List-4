<p><p>Chiral GC columns cater to various market types such as cellulose, cyclodextrin, macrocyclic antibiotics, protein, ligand exchange, prickle, and others. Cellulose columns separate enantiomers based on interactions with the cellulose molecule, while cyclodextrin columns use cyclodextrin derivatives as chiral selectors. Macrocyclic antibiotics columns contain chiral selectors derived from antibiotics, and protein columns utilize proteins as chiral selectors. Ligand exchange columns separate enantiomers based on ligand interactions, while prickle columns use specialized chiral selectors. Other market types include columns with unique chiral selectivity mechanisms.</p></p>
