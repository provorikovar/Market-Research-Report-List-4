<p><p>Handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) devices are increasingly being used in various medical applications due to their portability and ease of use. There are two main types of OCT devices in the market: Spectral-Domain OCT and Swept-Source OCT. Spectral-Domain OCT offers high resolution imaging and faster scan speeds, making it suitable for detailed imaging of tissue structures. Swept-Source OCT, on the other hand, provides deeper tissue penetration and is ideal for imaging structures like the choroid and retinal pigment epithelium. Both types of OCT devices are valuable tools in ophthalmology and other medical fields.</p></p>
