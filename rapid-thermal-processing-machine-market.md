<p><p>Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) machines are used for quick heating and cooling of semiconductor materials during manufacturing. There are two main types of RTP machines - lamp-based and laser-based. Lamp-based RTP machines use intense light to heat the materials, while laser-based machines use laser beams for heating. Lamp-based machines are more commonly used for larger scale production due to their cost-effectiveness, whereas laser-based machines are more precise and offer higher control over heating processes, making them ideal for research and development applications.</p></p>
