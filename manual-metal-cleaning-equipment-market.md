<p><p>Solvent metal cleaning equipment involves using solvents such as mineral spirits or acetone to remove dirt, oil, and grease from metal surfaces. This method is effective for heavy-duty cleaning but can be harmful to the environment. On the other hand, aqueous metal cleaning equipment uses water-based solutions to clean metal surfaces, making it a more environmentally friendly option. It is suitable for light to moderate cleaning tasks and is preferred for its eco-friendly nature. Both types of equipment are commonly used in industrial settings for metal cleaning applications.</p></p>
