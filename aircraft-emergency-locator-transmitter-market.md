<p><p>The Aircraft Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT) market can be divided into two main types: Embedded and Portable. Embedded ELTs are permanently installed in the aircraft and automatically activate in case of an emergency, transmitting distress signals to search and rescue authorities. Portable ELTs, on the other hand, can be manually activated by passengers or crew members during an emergency and are not permanently attached to the aircraft. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and security of aircraft in distress situations.</p></p>
