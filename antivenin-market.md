<p><strong>The Antivenin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyvalent</li><li>Monovalent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antivenin market is divided into two types: polyvalent and monovalent. Polyvalent antivenin is effective against multiple types of venom, making it a versatile choice for treating various snake bites. On the other hand, monovalent antivenin is specifically tailored to counteract the venom of a single snake species. While polyvalent antivenin offers broad coverage, monovalent antivenin provides targeted therapy for a specific type of snake bite. Healthcare providers may choose between these two options based on the patient's condition and the type of snake involved.</p></p>
