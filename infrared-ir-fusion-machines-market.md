<p><p>Infrared (IR) fusion machines are used for joining two pieces of plastic or thermoplastic pipes by melting the materials together. There are three main types of IR fusion machines: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. </p><p>Automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention for the fusion process. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input for set-up and operation. Manual machines require operators to control the fusion process entirely by hand. Each type of machine offers different levels of control and efficiency in the fusion process.</p></p>
