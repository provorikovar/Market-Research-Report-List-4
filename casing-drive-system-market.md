<p><p>Casing drive systems are used in various drilling operations and are categorized into three main types - Advancing Drilling System, Eccentric Drilling System, and Odex Drilling System. The Advancing Drilling System is used for accurate and efficient drilling in various soil conditions. The Eccentric Drilling System is used for drilling in unstable formations and hard rock layers. The Odex Drilling System is used for drilling where casing installation is needed simultaneously with drilling. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the drilling industry.</p></p>
