In the Beam Shaping (Top-Hat) market, DOEs are used to transform a Gaussian beam into a flat-top beam for uniform illumination. In the Beam Splitting market, DOEs are employed to split a single beam into multiple beams with precise control. Lastly, in the Beam Foci market, DOEs are utilized to focus or collimate light beams with high efficiency and accuracy. These markets cater to various applications in industries such as laser processing, microscopy, and telecommunications.</p></p>
