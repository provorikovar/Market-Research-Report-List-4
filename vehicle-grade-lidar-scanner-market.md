<p><p>Vehicle Grade LiDAR Scanner Market Types include Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses moving parts to scan the environment while Solid State LiDAR utilizes solid-state components like micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) to achieve the scanning process. Mechanical LiDAR systems are known for their high accuracy and long range capabilities but tend to be larger and more expensive. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR systems are more compact, lightweight, and affordable, making them ideal for mass market adoption in vehicles.</p></p>
