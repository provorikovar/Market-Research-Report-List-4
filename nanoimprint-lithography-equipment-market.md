<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Nanoimprint Lithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Embossing (HE)</li><li>UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL)</li><li>Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nanoimprint Lithography Equipment Market includes three main types:</p><p>1. Hot Embossing (HE): Involves heating a polymer mold and pressing it onto a substrate to create nanostructures. This method is cost-effective but has limited resolution.</p><p>2. UV-based Nanoimprint Lithography (UV-NIL): Uses UV light to cure a liquid resist on a substrate, creating high-resolution nanostructures. This method is versatile and offers excellent resolution.</p><p>3. Micro Contact Printing (µ-CP): Involves transferring ink from a stamp to a substrate to create nanostructures. This method is simple and low-cost, but has limited resolution.</p></p>
