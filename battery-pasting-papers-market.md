<p><ul><li>Cellulose Pasting Papers</li><li>Fiberglass Pasting Papers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery pasting papers are essential components in the manufacturing of batteries. There are two main types of pasting papers used in the battery industry: cellulose pasting papers and fiberglass pasting papers. Cellulose pasting papers are made from natural cellulose fibers and are known for their high absorption capacity and smooth surface. On the other hand, fiberglass pasting papers are made from fiberglass fibers and are preferred for their high strength and durability. Both types of pasting papers play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and longevity of batteries.</p></p>
