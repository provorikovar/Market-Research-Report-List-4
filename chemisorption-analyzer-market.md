<p><ul><li>Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzer</li><li>Static Chemisorption Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemisorption analyzers are used to study the surface properties of materials through the process of chemisorption. The two main types of Chemisorption analyzers are Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzers and Static Chemisorption Analyzers. Dynamic Flow analyzers involve the continuous flow of gases over a sample to study the adsorption and desorption processes. Conversely, Static analyzers involve the exposure of a sample to a gas for a specific period of time, followed by analysis. Both types of analyzers play a crucial role in understanding surface properties and catalytic processes in materials.</p></p>
