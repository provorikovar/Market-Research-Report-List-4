<p><p>Medical alarm systems come in three main types: Landline Type, Mobile Type, and Standalone Type. Mobile Type systems use cellular networks for communication, providing more flexibility for users who are away from home. Standalone Type systems do not require a landline or mobile phone and operate independently, making them suitable for those who do not have access to a phone or need a more portable solution.</p></p>
