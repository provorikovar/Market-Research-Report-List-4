<p><p>RS232 modems are categorized into two main market types: Synchronous and Asynchronous. Synchronous modems send data in a continuous stream at a fixed rate, ensuring data is transmitted and received in perfect alignment. Asynchronous modems, on the other hand, send data in small packets, allowing for more flexibility and efficient use of bandwidth. Both market types have their own advantages and are used in different scenarios based on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
