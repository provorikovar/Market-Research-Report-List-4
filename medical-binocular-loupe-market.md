<p><ul><li>TTL Loupes</li><li>Flip-up Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TTL loupes are mounted directly onto eyeglasses and provide a wider field of view with less distortion, making them ideal for detailed medical procedures. Flip-up loupes, on the other hand, have a hinge mechanism that allows the user to easily flip them up and out of the way when not in use. This type is preferred by individuals who need to switch between magnification levels frequently or prefer the added flexibility of being able to see without magnification when needed.</p></p>
