<p><p>The air conditioner accessories market consists of various components including refrigerants, compressors, condenser coils, expansion valves, and evaporator coils. The refrigerant market involves the sale and distribution of the cooling agents used in air conditioners. Compressors are responsible for circulating the refrigerant through the system. Condenser coils release heat from the refrigerant while the expansion valve regulates its flow. Lastly, the evaporator coil absorbs heat from indoor air, cooling it before circulating it back into the room.</p></p>
