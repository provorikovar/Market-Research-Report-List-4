<p><p>A laser gyroscope is a device that utilizes the principles of laser light to measure rotation rate and orientation. It consists of a laser beam split into two beams that are sent in opposite directions along a path and then recombined after reflecting off of rotating mirrors. The rotation of the gyro causes a phase shift between the two beams, which is measured to determine the rate of rotation.</p><p>The global Laser Gyroscope Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period. <p><p>Interferometric Fiber Optic Gyroscope utilizes the interference of light waves to measure rotation, offering high accuracy and reliability for navigation systems. On the other hand, Resonant Fiber Optic Gyroscope uses the principle of mechanical resonance to detect rotation, providing a compact and cost-effective solution for various applications. Both types of gyroscopes are widely used in aerospace, defense, and marine industries for precise navigation, stabilization, and pointing purposes due to their ability to measure angular velocity accurately.</p></p>
