<p><p>Metal finishing equipment market can be categorized into three types based on the type of finishing process involved: Inorganic metal finishing, Organometallic finishing, and Mixed metal finishing. Inorganic metal finishing involves processes such as electroplating and anodizing to improve the appearance and durability of metal surfaces. Organometallic finishing utilizes organic compounds containing metal elements for coating or plating metals. Mixed metal finishing combines both inorganic and organometallic processes to achieve desired properties in metal surfaces. Each type offers different benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
