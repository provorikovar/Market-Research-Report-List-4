<p><p>OLAP systems are classified into three main market types based on their architecture and processing capabilities. ROLAP (Relational OLAP) systems store data in relational databases and perform computations at query time. MOLAP (Multidimensional OLAP) systems store data in a multidimensional format for faster processing. Hybrid OLAP (HOLAP) combines features of both ROLAP and MOLAP systems. In addition to these three main types, there are other specialized OLAP systems designed for specific industries or use cases.</p></p>
