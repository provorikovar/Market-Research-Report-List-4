<p><strong>The Smartphone Display Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DDIC</li><li>TDDI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smartphone display driver market consists of two main types: DDIC (display driver integrated circuit) and TDDI (touch and display driver integration). DDICs are used to control the display functions of smartphones, such as color control and power management. TDDIs integrate both the display and touch functions into a single chip, reducing the number of components needed in a smartphone. Both DDIC and TDDI technologies play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of smartphone displays.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Smartphone Display Driver Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chip-On-Glass</li><li>Chip-On-Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smartphone display driver market application includes Chip-On-Glass (COG) and Chip-On-Film (COF) technologies. COG involves mounting the driver chip directly on the glass substrate of the display, reducing the overall size and thickness of the device. On the other hand, COF technology involves mounting the driver chip on a flexible film, allowing for increased flexibility and customization in display design. Both technologies are commonly used in smartphones to improve display performance and efficiency.</p></p>
