<p><p>Rubber Processing Machinery market includes various types such as Dispersion Mixer, Rubber Intensive Mixer, Rubber Rolling Mill, Kneader, and Granulator. Rubber Rolling Mills are used for refining and calendering rubber sheets. Kneaders provide effective mixing of rubber compounds. Granulators are used for shredding rubber scraps into smaller particles for further processing. These machinery types play crucial roles in the rubber processing industry.</p></p>
