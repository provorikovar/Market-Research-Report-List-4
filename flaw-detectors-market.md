<p><p>Flaw detectors market includes various types of equipment used for non-destructive testing to identify defects in materials. Magnetic flaw detectors use magnetic fields to detect surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Ultrasonic flaw detectors use high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects in a wide range of materials. Other types of flaw detectors may include eddy current or radiographic inspection tools. Each type of flaw detector offers unique advantages and is used in a variety of industries for quality control and safety assurance.</p></p>
