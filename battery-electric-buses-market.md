<p><ul><li>BEV</li><li>PHEV</li><li>FCEV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery-electric buses (BEV) are powered solely by electricity stored in a battery, eliminating emissions and reducing carbon footprint. Plug-in hybrid electric buses (PHEV) combine an electric motor and battery with a backup engine running on fossil fuels for longer range. Fuel cell electric buses (FCEV) use hydrogen to generate electricity, emitting only water vapor. These market types offer sustainable alternatives to traditional diesel buses, decreasing air pollution and promoting a cleaner environment.</p></p>
