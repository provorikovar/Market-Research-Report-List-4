<p><p>E-Beam Guns are devices used in vacuum deposition processes to create thin films on substrates. There are two main types of E-Beam Guns: Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns and Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns. Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns have multiple pockets, allowing for the deposition of different materials simultaneously, while Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns have only one pocket for the deposition of one material at a time. Both types of E-Beam Guns are used in various industries such as electronics, optics, and aerospace for precision coating applications.</p></p>
