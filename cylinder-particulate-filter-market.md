<p><p>A Cylinder Particulate Filter is a device used in diesel engines to reduce emissions of harmful particulate matter. It is designed to trap and remove soot particles from the exhaust gases before they are released into the atmosphere. The filter works by forcing the exhaust gases through a porous ceramic or metal filter, where the particles are trapped and burned off at high temperatures.</p><p>The Cylinder Particulate Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period.
