Autologous cellular immunotherapy involves using a patient's own cells to target and destroy cancer cells. CAR T-cell therapy utilizes genetically modified T cells to enhance their ability to recognize and attack cancer cells. Dendritic cell-based vaccine therapy involves using dendritic cells to stimulate the immune system to target cancer cells.
