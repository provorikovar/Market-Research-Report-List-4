<p><ul><li>Conventional Fluorescein Probes</li><li>Inorganic Ion Fluorescent Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence probes are used in various applications such as bioimaging, environmental monitoring, and drug discovery. Conventional fluorescein probes are organic dyes that emit a green fluorescence when excited by light. Inorganic ion fluorescent probes, on the other hand, are composed of metal ions and ligands that exhibit specific fluorescence properties in the presence of certain ions. Both types of probes are essential tools for research and diagnostics in various fields due to their ability to selectively detect and visualize specific molecules or ions.</p></p>
