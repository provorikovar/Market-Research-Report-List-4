<p><p>Heat pump water heaters come in three main types: Air Source Heat Pump, Water Source Heat Pump, and Ground Source Heat Pump. </p><p>Air Source Heat Pumps extract heat from the air and transfer it to water, making them suitable for most climates. Water Source Heat Pumps extract heat from a water source, such as a lake or river. Ground Source Heat Pumps extract heat from the ground and are highly efficient, but require a larger upfront investment. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different environments and applications.</p></p>
