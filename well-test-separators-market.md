<p><p>Well test separators are used in the oil and gas industry to separate different phases of fluids produced from a well during testing. The market includes different types such as TwoPhaseTestSeparator, ThreePhaseTestSeparator, and FourPhaseTestSeparator. Two phase separators separate gas and liquid, three phase separators separate gas, oil, and water, and four phase separators separate gas, oil, water, and solids. Each type is designed to efficiently separate and measure the different phases for accurate analysis of well productivity.</p></p>
