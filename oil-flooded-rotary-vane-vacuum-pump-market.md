<p><p>Oil flooded rotary vane vacuum pumps are categorized into two main types: single stage and two stage. Single stage rotary vane vacuum pumps are designed for applications where a lower level of vacuum is required, while two stage rotary vane vacuum pumps are used for applications that demand a higher level of vacuum. Single stage pumps have one set of vanes and one pumping stage, while two stage pumps have two sets of vanes and two pumping stages, enabling them to achieve higher levels of vacuum.</p></p>
