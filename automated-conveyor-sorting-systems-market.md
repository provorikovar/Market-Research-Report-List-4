<p><p>Automated Conveyor Sorting Systems are categorized into two main types: Linear Sortation Systems and Loop Sortation Systems. Linear systems are typically used for high-speed sorting of products in a straight line, while loop systems are designed for more complex sorting needs, allowing products to circulate through the system until they reach their destination. Both types of systems offer efficient and accurate sorting capabilities, improving overall productivity and reducing errors in the sorting process.</p></p>
