<p><p>Wheelchair medical equipment market can be broadly categorized into electric and manual wheelchairs. Electric wheelchairs are powered by batteries and offer the convenience of being maneuvered with minimal physical effort. They are suitable for users with limited upper body strength or mobility. On the other hand, manual wheelchairs require the user to propulse the chair using their own physical strength. They are generally lighter in weight and more affordable compared to electric wheelchairs. Both types cater to the diverse needs of individuals with mobility limitations.</p></p>
