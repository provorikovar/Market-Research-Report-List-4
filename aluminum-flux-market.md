<p><p>Aluminum flux market is divided into two types: resin-based and water-soluble. Resin-based fluxes are made from synthetic resins and organic compounds, providing excellent resistance to oxidation and ensuring a clean surface finish. Water-soluble fluxes, on the other hand, are environmentally friendly and can be easily removed with water after soldering. Both types of fluxes are used in aluminum soldering processes to improve wetting and bonding of the metal surfaces.</p></p>
