<p><p>High-performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) Market includes different types such as UV-Vis Detector (UVS), Fluorescence Detector (FD), Refractive Index Detector (RID), Electrochemical Detector (ED), and Conductivity Detector (CD). UVS works by detecting absorption of UV or visible light by the analyte. FD detects fluorescent compounds in the sample. RID measures changes in refractive index of the eluent. ED measures changes in current produced by redox reactions. CD measures changes in conductivity of the eluent. Each type offers unique advantages for analyzing different types of compounds in HPLC.</p></p>
