<p><strong>The Electric Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Way</li><li>Two Way</li><li>Three Way</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric control valves are categorized based on the number of ports they possess. Single way valves have one inlet and one outlet, allowing for simple on/off control. Two way valves have one inlet and one outlet, with the capability to modulate flow. Three way valves have one inlet and two outlets, enabling mixing or diverting of flow. Other types of control valves may have multiple ports for specific applications such as regulating pressure, temperature, or flow direction. These variations cater to different control needs in various industries.</p></p>
