<p><p>Flow rate liquid flow meters are devices used to measure the flow rate of liquids in various industries. They can be classified into two main types based on the viscosity of the liquid being measured: low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity flow meters are designed to accurately measure the flow rate of liquids with low thickness or resistance, while high viscosity flow meters are used for liquids that are thicker and more resistant to flow. These different types cater to the specific needs of industries working with liquids of varying viscosities.</p></p>
