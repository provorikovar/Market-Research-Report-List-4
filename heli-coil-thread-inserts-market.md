<p><p>Heli-Coil Thread Inserts are available in two main market types: Tanged Thread Inserts and Tangless Thread Inserts. Tanged inserts have a tang which is used to install the insert into the hole, providing stability during the installation process. Tangless inserts, on the other hand, do not have a tang and are simply installed using special tools, eliminating the need for tang removal after installation. Both types of inserts offer reliable thread reinforcement in a variety of materials and applications.</p></p>
