<p><p>Non-Woven Disc market is segmented into two main types: Quick Change Type and Arbor Hole Type. Quick Change Type discs are designed to easily attach and detach from power tools, allowing for quick and efficient disc changes. Arbor Hole Type discs, on the other hand, feature a center hole that fits onto a spindle or arbor of a power tool for secure and stable attachment. Both types offer versatility and convenience in various applications, making them popular choices in the abrasives industry.</p></p>
