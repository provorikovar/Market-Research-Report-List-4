<p><p>Elbow orthoses are devices designed to support and protect the elbow joint. Dynamic orthotics are adjustable and provide varying levels of support based on the individual's movement or activity. On the other hand, static orthotics maintain a fixed position to provide consistent support. Dynamic orthotics are more commonly used for conditions requiring flexibility and movement, while static orthotics are used for conditions requiring stability and immobilization. Both types of orthotics play a crucial role in the treatment and management of elbow injuries and conditions.</p></p>
