<p><p>Cloud seeding machines are used to enhance precipitation by dispersing substances into clouds. There are two main types of cloud seeding equipment: aerial and ground-based. Aerial cloud seeding equipment includes aircraft equipped with seeding devices that disperse seeding agents into clouds from above. Ground-based cloud seeding equipment includes generators and cannons that release seeding agents into the atmosphere from the ground. Both types of equipment aim to increase rainfall and mitigate drought conditions in regions where water scarcity is a concern.</p></p>
