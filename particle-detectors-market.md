<p><p>Particle detectors market types include ionization detectors, scintillation detectors, and others. Ionization detectors measure the ionization produced by particles passing through a gas or solid, while scintillation detectors detect the light produced by interactions between particles and a scintillating material. Other types of detectors include semiconductor detectors, Cherenkov detectors, and calorimeters. Each type of detector has unique properties and applications, making them suitable for different experiments and research purposes in various fields such as nuclear physics, particle physics, and medical imaging.</p></p>
