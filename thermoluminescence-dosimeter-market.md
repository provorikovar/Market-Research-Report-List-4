<p><p>Thermoluminescence dosimeter (TLD) is a device used for measuring ionizing radiation doses. It works by measuring the light emitted from a crystal when it is heated after being exposed to radiation. TLDs are commonly used in various industries, including healthcare, nuclear power plants, and research laboratories, to monitor the radiation exposure of workers and ensure their safety.</p><p>The Thermoluminescence Dosimeter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
