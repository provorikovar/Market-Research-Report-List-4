<p><p>SMT repair and inspection equipment market includes various types of machines used for detecting defects and ensuring quality in surface mount technology (SMT) processes. Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) machines are used for visual inspection of components and solder joints, while Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) machines check the quality of solder paste application. Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) machines utilize X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints and verify component placement. These types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring high-quality and reliable electronic products.</p></p>
