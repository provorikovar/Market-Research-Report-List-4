<p><p>Exercise pulleys are commonly categorized into two market types: dual cable pulleys and single cable pulleys. Dual cable pulleys feature two separate cables attached to the weights, allowing for more freedom of movement and range of motion during exercises. Single cable pulleys, on the other hand, have a single cable connected to the weights, typically requiring more stabilization and control during workouts. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different fitness preferences and needs.</p></p>
