<p><p>The Pyro-Fuse market includes two main types: Blade Fuse and Glass Tube Fuse. Blade Fuses are commonly used in automobiles and have a flat, blade-like design that can easily be inserted into a fuse holder. Glass Tube Fuses, on the other hand, have a cylindrical shape with a glass casing that allows for easy visual inspection of the fuse element. Both types of fuses serve the same purpose of protecting electrical circuits from overloads and short circuits, but they differ in design and application.</p></p>
