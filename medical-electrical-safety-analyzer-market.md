<p><p>The Medical Electrical Safety Analyzer Market consists of three main types: Hipot Tester, Dielectrimeter, and Megohmmeter. Hipot Tester is used to measure the insulation resistance of medical equipment, ensuring protection against electrical shock. Dielectrimeter is used to test the dielectric strength of electrical insulation, detecting any potential breakdowns. Megohmmeter measures the resistance of electrical insulation, identifying any defects that may cause electrical hazards. These devices are essential for maintaining the safety and reliability of medical equipment.</p></p>
