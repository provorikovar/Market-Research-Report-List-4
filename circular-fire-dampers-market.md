<p><p>Circular fire dampers come in two main types: manual fire dampers and motorized fire dampers. </p><p>Manual fire dampers require manual operation to adjust or shut off airflow in the event of a fire. They are more commonly used in smaller buildings where automation is not necessary.</p><p>Motorized fire dampers, on the other hand, are equipped with motors that automatically shut off airflow when a fire is detected. They are commonly used in larger buildings or facilities where there is a need for rapid response to fire emergencies.</p></p>
