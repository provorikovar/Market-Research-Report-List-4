<p><p>Zero phase current transformers are used for precise measurement of electrical currents. The split type market involves transformers that have the primary winding split into two sections, making it easier to install in existing systems. On the other hand, through type transformers have a primary winding that passes straight through the transformer. The split type is more suitable for retrofitting, while the through type is more commonly used in new installations due to its simpler design and ease of installation.</p></p>
