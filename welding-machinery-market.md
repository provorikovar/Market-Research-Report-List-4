<p><ul><li>Arc Welding Machine</li><li>Gas Welding/Cutting Machine</li><li>Resistance Welding Machine</li><li>Laser Welding Machine</li><li>Ultrasonic Welding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding machinery market is segmented into various types based on the welding technology used. Arc welding machines utilize electricity to create an arc between the electrode and the base material. Gas welding/cutting machines use a gas flame to melt and join metal parts. Resistance welding machines pass current through the parts to be joined. Laser welding machines use a highly focused beam of light to create a weld. Ultrasonic welding machines use high-frequency vibrations to join materials together.</p></p>
