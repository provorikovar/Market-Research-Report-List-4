<p><p>Resin injection guns come in two main types: manual and electric. Manual injection guns are operated by hand, requiring the user to physically pump the resin into the injection point. On the other hand, electric injection guns are powered by electricity and automate the process, making it easier and quicker to inject the resin. Both types of injection guns are commonly used in various industries for repairing cracks, filling gaps, and bonding materials together. The choice between manual and electric guns depends on the specific application and user preferences.</p></p>
