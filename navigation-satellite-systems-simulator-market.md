<p><ul><li>Single Channel Simulator</li><li>Multi Channel Simulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A single channel simulator is a navigation satellite system simulator that is capable of simulating the signals received from only one satellite at a time. It is suitable for testing and calibrating navigation devices that rely on a single satellite for positioning. On the other hand, a multi-channel simulator can simulate signals from multiple satellites simultaneously, providing a more complex and realistic testing environment for devices that utilize multiple satellites for navigation. Both types of simulators cater to different needs in the market for navigation satellite system testing and development.</p></p>
