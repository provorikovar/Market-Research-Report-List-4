<p><strong>The Rubber Filling Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Naphthenic Base</li><li>Paraffin Base</li><li>Aromatic Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber filling oil comes in three main types: naphthenic base, paraffin base, and aromatic base. Naphthenic base oils are known for their low viscosity and good solvency, making them ideal for rubber applications that require flexibility and resistance to heat and aging. Paraffin base oils offer good thermal stability and oxidation resistance, making them suitable for higher temperature applications. Aromatic base oils are valued for their high viscosity and compatibility with rubber compounds, making them a popular choice for tire manufacturing.</p></p>
