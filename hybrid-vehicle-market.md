<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid Vehicle</li><li>Series Hybrid Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid vehicles come in two main types: Parallel Hybrid Vehicle and Series Hybrid Vehicle. Parallel hybrids have both an internal combustion engine and an electric motor that work together to propel the vehicle, with the engine powering the wheels directly at times. On the other hand, series hybrids use the internal combustion engine to generate electricity, which then powers the electric motor to drive the wheels. Both types of hybrid vehicles aim to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions compared to traditional gasoline-powered vehicles.</p></p>
