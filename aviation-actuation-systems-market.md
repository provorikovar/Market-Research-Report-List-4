<p><p>Aviation Actuation Systems have three main types of drive systems: Hydraulic Drive System, Electric Drive System, and Pneumatic Drive System. </p><p>1. Hydraulic Drive System utilizes fluid power to control movements in aircraft systems. It is known for its high power density and reliability.</p><p>2. Electric Drive System utilizes electrical power for actuation. It offers precise control and energy efficiency.</p><p>3. Pneumatic Drive System uses compressed air to generate motion. It is commonly used for auxiliary functions and emergency systems in aircraft. </p><p>Each type has its specific advantages and applications in the aviation industry.</p></p>
