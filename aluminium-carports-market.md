<p><p>Aluminium carports are available in various types, with two common categories being Vertical Roof Carports and Regular Roof Carports. Vertical Roof Carports have panels that run from the peak of the structure down to the eaves, allowing for efficient water drainage and a more aesthetically pleasing appearance. On the other hand, Regular Roof Carports have panels that run horizontally along the length of the structure and are more cost-effective. Both types offer protection for vehicles from the elements and can be customized to suit the specific needs of the customer.</p></p>
