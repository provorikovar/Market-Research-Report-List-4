<p><p>A scanning contrast injector is a medical device used in imaging procedures to inject contrast agents into the body for better visualization of organs and blood vessels. The market for scanning contrast injectors is categorized into three types based on the number of chambers in the injector system: single chamber, two-chamber, and three-chamber. Single chamber injectors have one chamber for contrast agent, two-chamber injectors have separate chambers for contrast agent and saline, while three-chamber injectors have separate chambers for contrast agent, saline, and waste. Each type offers different functionalities and capabilities for medical professionals during imaging procedures.</p></p>
