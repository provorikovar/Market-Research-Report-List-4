<p><p>Magnets and magnetic materials market consists of three main types namely semi-hard magnets, soft magnets, and permanent/hard magnets. Soft magnets have low coercivity which allows them to be easily magnetized and demagnetized. Permanent or hard magnets retain their magnetization over a long period of time making them ideal for use in everyday consumer products. Each type caters to different industries and applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
