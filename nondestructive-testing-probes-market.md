<p><ul><li>Split Type Eddy Current Displacement Sensor</li><li>Integrated Eddy Current Displacement Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nondestructive Testing Probes come in different types, including Split Type Eddy Current Displacement Sensors and Integrated Eddy Current Displacement Sensors. Split Type probes have separate coils for excitation and sensing, providing more flexibility in positioning and usage. Integrated probes combine both excitation and sensing coils in a single unit, offering a more compact and user-friendly design. Both types are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for measuring and detecting defects in materials without causing damage.</p></p>
