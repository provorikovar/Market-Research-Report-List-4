<p><strong>The Industrial Fabric Looms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Jet Looms</li><li>Water Jet Looms</li><li>Rapier Looms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial fabric looms market can be broadly classified into three main types: Air Jet Looms, Water Jet Looms, and Rapier Looms. Air Jet Looms use compressed air to propel the weft yarn through the shed, making them faster and more efficient. Water Jet Looms use water as a medium to insert the weft yarn, suitable for weaving light to medium-weight fabrics. Rapier Looms have a rapier or flexible solid or hollow carrier to insert the weft yarn, making them versatile for weaving a variety of fabrics.</p></p>
