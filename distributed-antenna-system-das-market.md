<p><p>A Distributed Antenna System (DAS) is a network of antennas that helps improve wireless coverage and capacity indoors or outdoors. There are three main types of DAS markets:</p><p>1. Active DAS utilizes signal boosters and amplifiers to enhance wireless signals, making it suitable for large venues and buildings.</p><p>2. Passive DAS uses coaxial cables and antennas to distribute wireless signals without active components, making it cost-effective for small to medium-sized spaces.</p><p>3. Hybrid DAS combines elements of both active and passive DAS to provide optimal coverage and capacity for diverse environments.</p></p>
