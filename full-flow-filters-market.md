<p><strong>The Full-flow Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-chamber Filters</li><li>Twin-chamber Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full-flow filters are commonly found in two main types on the market - single-chamber filters and twin-chamber filters. Single-chamber filters consist of a single chamber housing both the filter element and the bypass valve. They are known for their simplicity and ease of installation. On the other hand, twin-chamber filters feature two separate chambers for the filter element and bypass valve. They are designed for more heavy-duty applications and provide better filtration efficiency and flow rates compared to single-chamber filters.</p></p>
