On-premise workforce management software is installed and maintained on the retailer's own servers and infrastructure, offering greater control and customization but also requiring more resources for maintenance. SaaS workforce management software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, offering more flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as retailers only pay for the services they use on a subscription basis.</p></p>
