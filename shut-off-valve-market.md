<p><p>Shut-off valve market types include ball valve, butterfly valve, gate valve, and globe valve. </p><p>Ball valves are rotary valves with a spherical closure that are commonly used in applications where tight shut-off is required. </p><p>Butterfly valves have a disc as the closing mechanism which is rotated to regulate flow. </p><p>Gate valves have a gate-like disc that moves up and down to control flow.</p><p>Globe valves have a plug that moves perpendicular to the flow path to regulate flow. Each type of valve serves a specific purpose in various industries.</p></p>
