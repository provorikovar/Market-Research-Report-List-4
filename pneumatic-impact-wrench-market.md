<p><p>The pneumatic impact wrench market consists of two main types: impact wrench and pulse pneumatic wrench. Impact wrenches deliver a high torque output through sudden and repetitive blows, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, pulse pneumatic wrenches provide more precise torque control by delivering short bursts of energy in quick succession. Both types of wrenches are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, and manufacturing where fastening and loosening of nuts and bolts is required.</p></p>
