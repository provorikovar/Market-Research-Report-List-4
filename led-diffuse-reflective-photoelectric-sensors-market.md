<p><p>LED diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors are available in two market types: digital signal and analog signal. Digital signal sensors provide a discrete output signal, typically a high or low voltage level, to indicate the presence or absence of an object. In contrast, analog signal sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies in proportion to the distance or intensity of the detected object. The choice between digital and analog signal sensors depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
