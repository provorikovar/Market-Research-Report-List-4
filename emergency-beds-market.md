<p><p>Emergency beds come in two main types: electric and manual. Electric emergency beds are equipped with motorized mechanisms that allow for easy adjustment of height, head, and feet positions with the push of a button. These beds are ideal for patients who require frequent adjustments and caregivers who need to easily move the bed. On the other hand, manual emergency beds require manual adjustments of height and positions using a crank or lever, making them more suitable for areas with limited access to electricity.</p></p>
