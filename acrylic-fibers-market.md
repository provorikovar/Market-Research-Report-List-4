<p><strong>The Acrylic Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Spinning Acrylic Fibers</li><li>Dry Spinning Acrylic Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic fibers market is categorized into two main types: Wet Spinning Acrylic Fibers and Dry Spinning Acrylic Fibers. Wet spinning involves extruding acrylic polymer solution through a spinneret into a coagulation bath, resulting in solidifying fibers. Dry spinning, on the other hand, involves dissolving the polymer in a solvent, extruding it into a chamber with hot air, and then solidifying the fibers as the solvent evaporates. Both methods produce different types of acrylic fibers with varying properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
