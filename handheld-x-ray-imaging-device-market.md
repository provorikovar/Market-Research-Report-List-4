<p><ul><li>DR</li><li>CR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld X-ray imaging devices are utilized in both the Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Radiography (CR) markets. DR technology uses digital flat-panel detectors to directly capture and produce high-quality images instantly, while CR technology transfers captured X-ray images onto a phosphor plate for processing. Both types offer portability and convenience in medical imaging, with DR being more advanced and faster, and CR being cost-effective and easier to upgrade. These devices play a crucial role in various medical settings for quick and efficient diagnostic imaging.</p></p>
