<p><p>Bronze sintered powder filters are classified into three types based on their porosity levels: low porosity (below 30%), medium porosity (30-60%), and high porosity (above 60%). Filters with low porosity offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for applications where fine filtration is required. Medium porosity filters strike a balance between filtration efficiency and flow rate. High porosity filters have excellent permeability, making them ideal for applications that require high flow rates. Each type caters to different filtration needs in various industries.</p></p>
