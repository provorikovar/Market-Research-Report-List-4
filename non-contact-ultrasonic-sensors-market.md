<p><p>Non-Contact Ultrasonic Sensors come in two main market types: Open Type and Sealed Type. The Open Type sensors are designed to have an open front surface, allowing them to detect objects without any barriers. They are suitable for applications where objects need to be detected from a distance. Sealed Type sensors, on the other hand, are enclosed to protect them from dust, water, and other environmental factors. They are typically used in industrial settings where harsh conditions are present.</p></p>
