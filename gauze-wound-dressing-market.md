<p><strong>The Gauze Wound Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Gauze</li><li>Nonwoven Gauze</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gauze wound dressing market is segmented into two main types: woven gauze and nonwoven gauze. Woven gauze dressings are constructed by weaving together threads to create a sturdy and absorbent fabric, making them ideal for moderate to heavy wound exudate. Nonwoven gauze, on the other hand, is made by bonding fibers together through a chemical or heat process, providing a softer and more conformable dressing for delicate or sensitive skin. Both types offer effective wound protection and absorption capabilities to promote healing.</p></p>
