<p><p>Offline Selective Soldering Systems are commonly used in two main market types - Small Batch Oven and Mass Production Oven. Small Batch Ovens cater to manufacturers who require flexible soldering solutions for low to medium volume production runs. These systems offer quick setup times and easy programming for various board sizes. On the other hand, Mass Production Ovens are designed for high-volume production where efficiency and speed are crucial. These systems are capable of handling continuous soldering operations with minimal downtime.</p></p>
