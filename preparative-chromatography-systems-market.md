<p><p>Preparative chromatography systems are classified into four main types based on the pressure range at which they operate. Ordinary pressure preparative chromatography systems are suitable for routine separations at lower pressures. Flash preparative chromatography systems are designed for rapid separations at high pressures. Medium pressure preparative chromatography systems offer a balance between speed and resolution. High pressure preparative chromatography systems are advanced systems capable of handling high-pressure separations for complex samples. Each type caters to different needs in the chromatography market.</p></p>
