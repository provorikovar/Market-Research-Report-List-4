<p><p>On-premise ERP systems are installed directly on a company's hardware and servers, requiring in-house IT management and maintenance. This market type offers greater control and customization, but can be costly and require significant resources.</p><p>Cloud-based ERP systems are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet. Cloud-based ERP systems also require less technical expertise and maintenance from the company.</p></p>
