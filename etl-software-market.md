<p><p>ETL software comes in two main types - cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based ETL software allows for data integration and transformation to be done on the cloud, providing flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises ETL software is installed and managed within the organization's own infrastructure, offering more control over data security and customization. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences, with cloud-based solutions being popular for their cost-effectiveness and accessibility, while on-premises solutions are favored for their security and compliance.</p></p>
