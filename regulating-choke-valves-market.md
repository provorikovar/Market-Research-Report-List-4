<p><p>Regulating choke valves are classified into three main types: needle and seat type, multiple orifice valve, and cage valve. The needle and seat type choke valve is designed with a needle that can be adjusted to control the flow of fluid. The multiple orifice valve has several small holes that can be opened or closed to regulate flow. The cage valve uses a cage-shaped structure to regulate flow. Each type of regulating choke valve offers different benefits and is used in various applications based on their unique design and operating principles.</p></p>
