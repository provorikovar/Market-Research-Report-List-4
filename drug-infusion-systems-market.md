<p><p>Drug infusion systems market includes various types such as ambulatory infusion systems, implantable infusion systems, and syringe infusion systems. Ambulatory infusion systems are portable devices that allow patients to receive medication while on the go. Implantable infusion systems are surgically implanted devices that deliver medication directly into the body. Syringe infusion systems are used to deliver precise amounts of medication through a syringe pump. Each type of infusion system caters to different patient needs and preferences, providing efficient and convenient drug delivery solutions.</p></p>
