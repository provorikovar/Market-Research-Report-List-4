<p><p>Bicycle-sharing systems can be categorized into three main market types: Point-to-Point, Ride-and-Return, and Distributed market. Point-to-Point systems allow users to pick up and drop off bicycles at any designated station. Ride-and-Return systems require users to return the bicycle to the same station they rented it from. Distributed market systems operate without fixed stations, allowing users to pick up and drop off bikes anywhere within a designated area. Each market type offers different levels of flexibility and convenience for users.</p></p>
