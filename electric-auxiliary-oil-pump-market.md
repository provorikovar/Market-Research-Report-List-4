<p><p>An Integrated Pump in the Electric Auxiliary Oil Pump market refers to a system where the pump is installed directly within the engine or transmission, providing a more compact and streamlined solution. On the other hand, a Separate Pump refers to a standalone unit that operates independently from the main engine system, offering flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types cater to different industry needs, with Integrated Pumps being ideal for space-saving and efficiency, while Separate Pumps offer versatility and ease of replacement.</p></p>
