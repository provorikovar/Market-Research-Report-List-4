<p><p>Presbyopia-correcting IOLs are a type of intraocular lens implants designed to help individuals with presbyopia, a condition causing difficulty in focusing on near objects as they age. There are three main types of presbyopia-correcting IOLs: Multifocal IOLs, which provide vision at multiple distances; Extended Depth of Focus IOLs, which offer a continuous range of vision from near to far; and Accommodative IOLs, which mimic the eye's natural ability to change focus. These options provide individuals with greater flexibility and improved vision post cataract surgery.</p></p>
