<p><p>Acne medicine is available in two main markets: over-the-counter (OTC) and prescription medicine. OTC options typically include creams, gels, and lotions containing ingredients like benzoyl peroxide or salicylic acid. These products are easily accessible in pharmacies and are suitable for mild to moderate acne. Prescription medications, on the other hand, are recommended for severe cases and may include oral antibiotics, retinoids, or hormonal treatments. They are specifically prescribed by a dermatologist or healthcare provider after assessing the individual's skin condition and needs.</p></p>
