<p><strong>The Industrial CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial CMOS image sensors are classified into two main types based on their architecture: Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI). FSI sensors have the light-sensitive elements located on the front side of the sensor, while BSI sensors have these elements on the back side, resulting in improved light sensitivity and image quality. FSI sensors are more commonly used in industrial applications due to their lower cost, while BSI sensors are gaining popularity for their superior performance in low-light conditions.</p></p>
