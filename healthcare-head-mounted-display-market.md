<p><p>Healthcare Head Mounted Display market includes two main types - Helmet Mounted Display and Eye Wear Display. Helmet Mounted Display refers to a display system attached to a helmet or headgear, providing hands-free access to visual information. Eye Wear Display, on the other hand, involves a display mounted directly on a pair of eyeglasses or goggles, allowing for a more lightweight and compact solution. Both types offer healthcare professionals the ability to access important medical data and information in real-time during procedures, improving efficiency and patient outcomes.</p></p>
