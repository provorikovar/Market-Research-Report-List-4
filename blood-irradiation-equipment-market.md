<p><p>There are two main types of blood irradiation equipment on the market: X-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment and Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment. X-ray equipment uses electromagnetic radiation to irradiate blood, while gamma-ray equipment uses gamma radiation. Both types are commonly used in hospitals and blood banks to prevent transfusion-associated graft versus host disease and to treat blood disorders. The choice between X-ray and gamma-ray equipment depends on factors such as facility requirements, cost, and specific application needs.</p></p>
