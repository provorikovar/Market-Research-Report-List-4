<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial metallic paints come in two main types - solvent based and water based. Solvent based metallic paints are known for their durability and strong bonding properties. They are best suited for outdoor applications and areas where there is high exposure to chemicals and harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, water based metallic paints are known for their low odor and environmental friendliness. They are ideal for indoor applications and areas with poor ventilation. Both types offer a wide range of colors and finishes to suit different industrial needs.</p></p>
