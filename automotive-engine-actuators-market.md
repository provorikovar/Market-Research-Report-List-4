<p><p>Automotive Engine Actuators market includes VVT System and Electronic Throttle System. VVT System (Variable Valve Timing) helps optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency by adjusting the timing of the opening and closing of the engine's valves. Electronic Throttle System, also known as drive-by-wire system, replaces traditional mechanical linkages between the accelerator pedal and the throttle with electronic sensors. This allows for more precise control of engine power and improves overall vehicle response. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing engine performance and efficiency in modern vehicles.</p></p>
