<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knotless suture anchors are used in orthopedic surgeries to secure soft tissues to bone without the need for tying knots. They are available in absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable suture anchors gradually degrade in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. Non-absorbable suture anchors remain in the body permanently and provide long-lasting support. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the specific surgical needs of the patient, providing surgeons with options to choose the most suitable product for each case.</p></p>
