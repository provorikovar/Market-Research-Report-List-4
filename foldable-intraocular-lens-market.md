<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs</li><li>Other Foldable IOLs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foldable Intraocular Lens (IOL) market is divided into three main types: Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs, Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs, and Other Foldable IOLs. Hydrophobic Acrylic IOLs are known for their resistance to water, reducing the risk of developing opacification over time. Hydrophilic Acrylic IOLs offer excellent biocompatibility and reduced risk of inflammatory responses. Other Foldable IOLs include options made from different materials like silicone or PMMA. Each type offers unique benefits and considerations for patients undergoing cataract surgery.</p></p>
