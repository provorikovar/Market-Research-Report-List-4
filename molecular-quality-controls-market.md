<p><ul><li>Independent</li><li>Instrument Specific</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Independent Molecular Quality Controls are designed to work across a range of different molecular testing platforms and are not specific to any one instrument. They provide a universal quality control solution that can be used across multiple laboratories and instruments. On the other hand, Instrument-Specific Molecular Quality Controls are tailored to work with a specific type or brand of molecular testing instrument. These controls are optimized to mimic the samples typically run on that specific instrument, ensuring accurate and reliable results.</p></p>
