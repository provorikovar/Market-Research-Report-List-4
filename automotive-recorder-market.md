<p><p>The automotive recorder market can be divided into two main types: single channel and multi-channel recorders. Single channel recorders are designed to record data from one specific source, such as a single camera or sensor. These recorders are commonly used for basic recording needs in vehicles. On the other hand, multi-channel recorders are capable of recording data from multiple sources simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the vehicle's surroundings. Multi-channel recorders are often used in advanced automotive applications where a more detailed recording is required.</p></p>
