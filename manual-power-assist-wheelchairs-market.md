<p><p>Manual Power-Assist Wheelchairs come in two main types - Powerpack Type and Hand Bike Type. Powerpack Type wheelchairs have an additional motorized device that attaches to the wheels of a manual wheelchair, providing extra power to assist with propulsion. On the other hand, Hand Bike Type wheelchairs have a separate hand-operated system that can be attached to the front of the wheelchair, allowing the user to pedal and propel themselves forward using their upper body strength. Both types offer increased mobility and independence for users.</p></p>
