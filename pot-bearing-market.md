<p><strong>The Pot Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Sliding Pot Bearing</li><li>Fixed Pot Bearing</li><li>Guided Pot Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pot bearing market includes three types - free sliding pot bearing, fixed pot bearing, and guided pot bearing. Free sliding pot bearings allow movement in all directions, fixed pot bearings restrict movement in all directions, and guided pot bearings allow movement in specific directions while restricting movement in others. These different types cater to various structural requirements and are used in applications where controlled movement and load transfer are essential. Overall, pot bearings play a crucial role in the functioning and stability of various structures.</p></p>
