<p><p>Pulse solenoid valves are commonly categorized into two main types: Embedded Pulse Solenoid Valves and Straight Through Pulse Solenoid Valves. Embedded valves are compact and directly integrated into a system, often used in applications where space is limited. On the other hand, Straight Through valves have a linear flow path, allowing for high flow rates and minimal pressure drop. Both types of valves are widely used in various industries such as automotive, medical, and HVAC for precise control of fluid flow.</p></p>
