<p><strong>The Aircraft Tire Retreading Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Cure</li><li>Mold-Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft tire retreading market is divided into two main types - Pre-Cure and Mold-Cure. In Pre-Cure retreading, a new tread is applied to the tire casing before it is cured, while in Mold-Cure retreading, the tread is applied after curing. Pre-Cure retreading is known for enhanced tread life and fuel efficiency, while Mold-Cure retreading offers better performance in terms of heat resistance and durability. Both methods are widely used in the aviation industry to extend the life of aircraft tires and reduce costs.</p></p>
