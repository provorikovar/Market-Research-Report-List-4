<p><p>Passive sonar systems can be categorized into three main types based on the frequency at which they operate: Low Frequency (LF), Medium Frequency (MF), and High Frequency (HF). LF sonars are ideal for long-range detection and tracking of underwater targets, MF sonars are suited for mid-range operations with higher resolution, and HF sonars are designed for short-range detection with maximum precision. Each market type serves specific requirements and applications in maritime surveillance, defense, and research sectors.</p></p>
