<p><p>Fixed grounding indicators are mounted in a specific location such as a control panel or switchboard. They are designed for permanent installation and provide continuous monitoring of grounding status. Portable grounding indicators, on the other hand, are designed for temporary use and can be easily moved from one location to another. They are typically used in field applications or during maintenance activities. Both types of grounding indicators serve the same purpose of ensuring safe and effective grounding of electrical systems.</p></p>
