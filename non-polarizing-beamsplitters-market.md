<p><p>Non-polarizing beamsplitters are optical components that split a beam of light into two separate beams without affecting the polarization state of the light. There are two main types of non-polarizing beamsplitters: non-polarizing plate beamsplitters and non-polarizing cube beamsplitters. Plate beamsplitters use a thin optical coating to split the light, while cube beamsplitters use a cubic prism to achieve the same effect. Both types are commonly used in various optical systems and applications for their high performance and durability.</p></p>
