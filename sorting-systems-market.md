<p><ul><li>Tilt-tray</li><li>Cross-belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sorting systems play a crucial role in streamlining operations in various industries. Two common types of sorting systems are tilt-tray and cross-belt systems. Tilt-tray systems use trays that tilt to drop items onto the designated sorting chute. This system is ideal for handling a wide range of products efficiently. On the other hand, cross-belt systems use belts that move items across different sorting stations. These systems are known for their high speed and accuracy in sorting items, making them suitable for high-volume sorting operations.</p></p>
