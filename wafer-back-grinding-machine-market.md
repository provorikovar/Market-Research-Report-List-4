Wafer edge grinding machines focus on trimming the edges of the wafer, while wafer surface grinding machines are used to grind the front and back surfaces of the wafer to achieve the desired thickness and smoothness. Both types play a crucial role in the production of high-quality semiconductor devices.</p></p>
