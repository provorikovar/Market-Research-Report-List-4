<p><p>Antithrombotic Treatment Market includes different types of medications used to prevent blood clots. Some of the major categories include NOACs (novel oral anticoagulants), Heparin, Warfarin, and other miscellaneous anticoagulants. NOACs are newer anticoagulants that work directly on specific factors in the blood clotting cascade. Heparin is an injectable anticoagulant used in hospitals. Warfarin is a traditional oral anticoagulant that requires regular monitoring. Other antithrombotic medications include antiplatelet drugs and synthetic anticoagulants. Each type has its own benefits and risks for different patient populations.</p></p>
