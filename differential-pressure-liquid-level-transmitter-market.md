<p><p>Differential Pressure Liquid Level Transmitters are commonly available in Straight Rod Type, Flange Type, and Screw-Type variations. Straight Rod Type transmitters are designed with a straight rod sensor for easy installation in small tanks. Flange Type transmitters are equipped with flanges for secure mounting on larger tanks. Screw-Type transmitters feature a screw-in design for quick and simple installation. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific application requirements, such as tank size, pressure levels, and accessibility.</p></p>
