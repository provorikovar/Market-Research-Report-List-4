<p><p>Assembly automation refers to the use of various types of equipment and systems to automate production processes in manufacturing industries. The market is divided into three main types: robot automation equipment, other automation equipment, and central control systems. Robot automation equipment includes robotic arms and machines that perform tasks like welding or material handling. Other automation equipment includes conveyors, sensors, and vision systems. Central control systems coordinate and control the operation of all automation equipment in the assembly line for seamless production.</p></p>
