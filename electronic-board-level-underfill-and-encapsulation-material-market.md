<p><ul><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Capillary Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li><li>Wafer level Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Board Level Underfill and Encapsulation Material Market Types are categorized into No Flow Underfill, Capillary Underfill, Molded Underfill, and Wafer level Underfill. No Flow Underfill is a pre-cured material that does not flow during the curing process. Capillary Underfill is a low viscosity material that flows into narrow gaps by capillary action. Molded Underfill is a pre-cured material that is molded around electronic components. Wafer level Underfill is applied at the wafer level before the components are singulated. Each type serves different purposes in electronic board assembly.</p></p>
