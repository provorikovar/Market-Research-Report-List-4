<p><p>Thermoforming clear aligner market is categorized into three main types based on the material used: TPU, PETG, and Others. TPU clear aligners are known for their flexibility and strength, making them ideal for complex dental cases. PETG aligners are durable and clear, providing a comfortable fit for patients. The "Others" category includes various materials such as PMMA and PEEK, which offer unique properties like stain resistance or biocompatibility. Each type of clear aligner material caters to different patient needs and preferences in the orthodontic industry.</p></p>
